Chris Evans




Chris Evans Webpage

Chris Evans is an American actor, film producer, and director. He was born on June 13, 1981 in Boston, Massachusetts. Evans made small appearenses in minor tv roled in the early 2000's. His breakthrough performance was a superhero movie called Fantastic Four. He is best known for being Steve Rodgers (Captain America) in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. He played Captain America for 10 films debuting in 2011 (The First Avenger) and his last in 2019 (Avengers: Endgame). His work in this franchise lead him to be one of the worlds highest paid actors.
